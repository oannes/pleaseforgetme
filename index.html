You have a right to be forgotten

Perhaps you want to start a new life, have a fresh start before a job interview or remove the digital footprints of a family member who has passed away. PleaseForget.me is a site that provides simple instructions to remove your virtual presence.

To be forgotten:

1. Remove your images from the web.

Make a Google image search and Bing image search for your complete name and other names you have used online. Copy and paste all the links of the images to a text file. Contact webmaster of every site to remove your photos (webmaster is the person responsible of the site; you can typically reach her from contact@nameofthesite.com or webmaster@nameofthesite.com).

After you have contacted every webmaster, you can use Google’s automated tool and Bing’s contact form.

2. Remove your Facebook account.

  • Log in to Facebook.
  • Delete all your friends from your friend list
  • Delete your pictures
  • Delete your comments you have left for your friends
  • Leave all the groups and pages you have joined and unlike all the pages you have liked
  • Completely delete your Facebook account

3. Deactivate your Twitter account

4. Close your LinkedIn account

  • From homepage, click your photo and select “Privacy and settings”
  • Click Account
  • Under the Helpful Links section, choose “Close your account”.

5. Delete other social media accounts

  • Tumblr. Go to this link.
  • Delete your Pinterest account.
  • Delete your instagram account
  • Delete Flickr account
  • If you need to remove other accounts, consult http://www.accountkiller.com/

6. Delete your Google, Microsoft and Yahoo accounts.

Notice that if you use Outlook, Yahoo mail or Gmail, they will stop operating.

  • Delete your Yahoo account
  • Delete your Microsoft account by going to the link and clicking “Close account”.
  • Delete your Google account

To finally be forgotten

Google and Bing your name and any other names you could have used in the web. Act accordingly to remove your accounts and information from the web. You can also use Duckduckgo.com to search beyond your filter bubble.

Notice that many services wait up to a month until they actually remove your account. Schedule an event in a month to again Google and Bing your name. You have the right to be forgotten: remove any traces by contacting relevant webmasters.
